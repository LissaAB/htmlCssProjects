Two Truths, One Lie

Here are some notes for the Two Truths, One Lie about me. Let's see if you can figure it out.

Atlas

My Great Great Uncle was Charles Atlas, an Italian-born American bodybuilder best remembered as the developer of a bodybuilding method and its associated exercise program which spawned a landmark advertising campaign featuring his name and likeness.

Monsters and Aliens

I like to draw monsters and aliens in my free time. A notable painting was of a serial killer alien, however, my favorite drawing is of monsters trying to change a lightbulb.

Jail Time

I once spent a night in jail for mistaken identity. Police arrested me for stealing a VHS tape from Blockbusters. They thought it was me because I'm only 4'10" and I matched the height and looked similar to the person who actually did it. Eventually I was able to corroborate my whereabouts. I had been in a nearby store, CVS, at the time and thankfully the checkout lady remembered me by the conversation we had about the gum choices.
